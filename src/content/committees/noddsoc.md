# Introduction

NoDDSoc is the University of Sheffield's TTRPG society, and is one of the oldest and largest societies in Sheffield!
The society holds regular socials, collabs and one-shots in addition to the weekly campaigns run by our Game Masters.

## Responsibilities

- Managing the society's website
- Ensuring the society's accounts are secure and accessible
- Teaching Committee how to use the website and accounts
- Managing the society's Discord servers
- Managing the society's Google Drive and Calendar
